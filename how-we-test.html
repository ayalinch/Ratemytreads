Overview

We combine controlled evaluations with real-world trials. Each pair is reviewed on traction, stability, and usable design factors (fit, warmth, water handling). Scores are normalized to a 0–20 traction index, then mapped to simple tiers using the legend below.

Legend

Equipment

  • Ice/winter surface lane with temperature control
  • Harness for safety during slip trials
  • Task markers for walk / stop / turn cycles

Protocol

  1. Warm-up and sizing check
  2. Sequence: walk → heel strike → turn → stop
  3. Repeat runs; log slips and recoveries
