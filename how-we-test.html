Overview

We combine controlled evaluations with real-world trials. Each pair is reviewed on traction, stability, and usable design factors (fit, warmth, water handling). Scores are normalized to a 0–20 traction index, then mapped to tiers using the legend below. We present two parallel systems: Snow/Ice and Wet/Rain.

Legend

  • Very High (≥15)

    Confident traction; strong stop/turn stability.

  • High (11–14)

    Reliable grip in most winter sidewalks.

  • Medium (7–10)

    OK on packed snow; be cautious on smooth ice.

  • Low (<7)

    Limited traction; avoid smooth ice without aids.

Equipment

  • Ice/winter surface lane with temperature control
  • Harness for safety during slip trials
  • Task markers for walk / stop / turn cycles

Protocol

  1. Warm-up and sizing check
  2. Sequence: walk → heel strike → turn → stop
  3. Repeat runs; log slips and recoveries
