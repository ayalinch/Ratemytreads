Overview

We combine controlled lane trials with real-world walks. Each pair is scored on traction and stability, with notes on fit, warmth, and water handling. Results are normalized to a 0–20 index and mapped to tiers using the legend. We publish two systems: Snow/Ice (❄) and Wet/Rain (💧).

Test set

  • Surfaces: smooth ice, packed snow, wet pavement
  • Tasks: walk → heel-strike → turn → stop
  • Design checks: outsole compound, lug depth & pattern, optional studs

Legend

  • Very High (≥15)

    Confident traction; strong stop/turn stability.

  • High (11–14)

    Reliable grip in most winter sidewalks.

  • Medium (7–10)

    OK on packed snow; be cautious on smooth ice.

  • Low (<7)

    Limited traction; avoid smooth ice without aids.

Equipment

  • Temperature-controlled ice/snow lane
  • Safety harness during slip trials
  • Markers for walk / turn / stop cycles

Protocol

  1. Fit check & warm-up
  2. Tasks: walk → heel-strike → turn → stop
  3. Repeat runs; log slips & recoveries
